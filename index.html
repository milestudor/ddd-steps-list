How the Commanders became like that out of nowhere

An incredible 2024 draft from Adam Peters set the Commanders up great for the upcoming season

  • The additions of players like Jayden Daniels, Mike Sainristil, Brandon Coleman, and Jer'Zhan Newton had immediate impacts for this football team

The next step this front office took to improving the roster was to find quality talent in free agency

  • Signing key veterans in Bobby Wagner and Zach Ertz, and landing a few splash signings in Frankie Luvu, Jeremy Chinn, and more helped the Commanders immensely.

The improvement of Jayden Daniels transformed not only the team but also brought back the football culture that the DMV area had been missing

  • In just his second game, Jayden Daniels announced himself to the world with an absolutely sublime performance against the Bengals on Monday Night Football. This was where the legend was born.
